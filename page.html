Abandoned Galaxy -
is turn-based strategy game where a player has only one goal - to defeat all other players
  • 50 levels
  • Map editor. Create your own maps!
  • Local Multiplayer. Play with your friends up to 5 people (or AI players) on one device!
  • 3 difficulty levels
  • 4 types of planets
  • Lite version has 5 levels and one slot for your own map
